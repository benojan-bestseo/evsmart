Our Technology

The Layer-0 infrastructure for self-sufficiency and resilience

Our developments are based on physical facts, mathematical logic and decades of industrial know-how. We combine proven principles into systems that the free market is desperately seeking. Since these are protected innovations, we do not provide deep design data publicly here, but summarise our two core areas in hard facts:

Core area 1

Self-sufficient energy technology (power4you energy systems)

The blueprint for global energy freedom.

We liberate highly efficient combined heat and power from the shackles of the central power grid. With an efficiency of 98 %, our systems generate heat and power using absolute fuel flexibility (from biogenic liquid gas/LPG to hydrogen) directly at the consumer's location (off-grid).

Core area 2

Intelligent networks & Smart Solutions

The basis of any genuine digitalisation & The strategic backbone.

Previous digitalisation systems operate in fragmented, passive silos and are extremely vulnerable in a crisis. We are finally tearing down these walls. Our Smart Solutions, the self-sufficient mesh network and the hardware of the “Yellow Box” form the absolute foundation for genuine, functioning digitalisation.

The foundation of this revolution is formed by the “Yellow Box” – a physical industrial node that processes sensor data on site in real time using edge computing – completely independent of external servers, internet, mobile communications or WLAN.

  • The universal basis: The indestructible foundation (Layer 0) for Smart Home, Smart Building, Smart Factory, logistics, Smart Marina and military.
  • The digital protective shield: As a resilient safeguard, our hybrid mesh network guarantees safe operation during power failures, cyber attacks and hybrid threats.
  • Transformation without standstill: The module is attached directly to the existing electrics and establishes immediate connectivity. No expensive rewiring.
  • Validated under extreme conditions: From the laboratory phase (TRL 4) up to operational validation at TRL 9 in genuine live operation (such as in the maritime security sector at Stolt Tankers).
Our Economic Cycle

The power of the community & Our economic cycle

Large communities and automobile clubs lead by example: When customers, users and partners join forces, an unshakeable foundation is created. We unite all stakeholders in a closed membership structure.

Bundling of purchasing power

Following the model of large cooperatives, we purchase primary energy, raw materials and central services jointly. This allows us to achieve extremely favourable terms that would be unattainable for individuals.

Tax optimisation & use of profits

Members work together internally in a tax-optimised manner (no classic third-party sales). Surpluses flow directly back to the members as benefits, rather than being distributed to shareholders subject to tax.

Reduction of bureaucracy & security

We are free from rigid trade restrictions, pool international payment transactions simply and protect our intellectual property collectively.
