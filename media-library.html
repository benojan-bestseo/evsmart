Chapter 6 · IP Protection & NDA Pipeline

The two-pillar architecture of the website

(Legal security & IP protection) — The entire homepage is divided into two strictly separated worlds according to the principle of unconditional protection of our intellectual property (IP protection according to § 16 of the statutes):

Area 1

The public area (Freely accessible)

This area is freely visible to every visitor, the Salzburg tax office, auditors and the State Police Directorate. It serves exclusively for the statutory disclosure obligation and proof of the state existence of the association.

  • The identity & contact data (Imprint obligation acc. to § 25 MedienG / § 5 ECG): Full association name according to the register, ZVR number 1740481664, official delivery address and the named executive committee.
  • The official transparency centre: Free download of statutes, register of associations extract and status notification.
  • The public media showcase: The home page with mission and forms for supporting members, B2B partners or investors. Teaser headings of the media library – but absolutely without technical details, prices, figures or data.
Area 2

The internal area (Strictly confidential – AUTOMATED NDA STRUCTURE)

Here lies the entire gold of the association. No person receives access to any of these documents before they have proven their identity and electronically signed a legally binding non-disclosure agreement (NDA acc. to § 16 para. 11 of the statutes).

The fully automated system mechanics (The process):

  • The registration: The visitor fills out the application form of the media library (Name, company name, email).
  • The automatic email dispatch: The system fully automatically generates an email containing the NDA as an electronic signature document (e.g. via DocuSign / Adobe Sign).
  • The digital signature: The visitor signs the NDA digitally.
  • The automatic activation: Only after a successful signature does the system activate the protected link for the media library or send the access data for the login area.

1. The Media Library / The Info Centre (Behind the automated NDA barrier):

  • Pitch Deck 1 (power4you energy systems): All data on the 98 % efficiency, the Home & Business Edition on pallet scale, the €2,500 unit economics, prices and franchise model.
  • Pitch Deck 2 (Smart Solutions & Layer-0): All data on the physical Yellow Box, the autonomous mesh network without internet/mobile communications and edge computing.
  • Project Guide & Reports: Detailed use cases and validation reports of the Smart Maritime Security in live operation at TRL 9 (e.g. Stolt Tankers).
  • The investment plan: Strategic allocation of capital (> €3 million) for series production (CAPEX), sourcing and the leveraging through public funding.

2. The internal Governance & Compliance Files (Strictly secret in the protected login area):

  • The complete, original rules of procedure (GO) of the association with internal financial responsibilities.
  • All official, uncensored application documents for EU and NATO funding programmes.
  • The detailed member analysis and target group strategies.
  • The statutory Conflict-of-Interest Register and the WiEReG compliance package.
