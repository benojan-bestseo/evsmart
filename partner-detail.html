Overview

Alchemy was founded in January 2016, specialising in IoT and Smart Systems. With a unique in-house developed infrastructure, Alchemy delivers tailored software and hardware solutions across diverse markets. Their focus is on meeting innovation and excellence with simplicity, underpinned by expertise in IoT and Cybersecurity.

They provide solutions for Public Institutions, the Defense Industry, and the Private Sector, with a strong emphasis on research and development – including projects in Industrial IoT 4.0, automation, factory management, and cybersecurity.

Current focus areas

  • Yellow Box: Joint project enhancing vehicle connectivity for safer roads – directly connected to our Layer-0 / Smart Solutions core area.
  • NFVI & IoT with Vodafone: Network functions virtualisation, securing and managing IoT devices across 4G, 5G and 6G networks.
  • Cybersecurity for the Electric Vehicle Ecosystem: Focused on securing electric vehicle charging stations.
